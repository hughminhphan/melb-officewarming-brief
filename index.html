2. About Lyra

Lyra Technologies is a digital product studio based in Australia. We pair founding-calibre engineers with startups and scale-ups to build products.

  • Tagline: "Founding engineers for 75+ SV startups"
  • Team: 70+ people across Sydney (Barangaroo) and Melbourne (Collins St)
  • Clients: 75+ projects, 10+ YC companies
  • Engineers from: Canva, Eucalyptus, Instagram, YC-backed startups
  • Culture: Young, high-energy, builder mentality. Chess clubs, bouldering, cafe runs, shipping code fast.

The Melbourne office is newer and growing fast. The energy should feel like "we've arrived", not "we're corporate."

3. Brand Guidelines

Typography

Satoshi is the only Lyra brand font. Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500). Get it from Fontshare.

Do NOT use Space Grotesk, Basement Grotesque, or Helvetica Light. Those belong to a separate personal brand, not Lyra.

Colour Palette

#000000Background (black)
#010314Background alt (dark navy)
#D4D4D6Primary text
#9E9EA0Secondary text
#B59BFCAccent purple (key brand colour)
#9E48FE > #2C42E6Gradient accent (CTAs, highlights)
#421E6BDeep purple
#1D1D22Card / surface
#FAF9F5Warm off-white (use sparingly)

Key principle: Dark, minimal, premium. Lots of negative space. Subtle glassmorphism. Boldness from ONE element at a time.

Logo

Included in the Google Drive folder:

  • logo-white.png (981x360) - use on dark backgrounds
  • logo-black.png (981x360) - use on light backgrounds
  • logo-icon.png (330x330) - icon only

Logo should be present but not dominant. Corner placement. Let the event messaging be the hero.

Visual Style

  • Dark, minimal, premium. Clean layouts, generous whitespace.
  • Glassmorphism accents (subtle frosted glass, translucent borders)
  • Border radius: 12px (rounded, not pill)
  • Use real team/office photos. NO stock photos.

5. Mood and Direction

Celebratory but not corporate. Think "house party invite designed by a tech company", not corporate event flyer.

  • Warm - genuinely invited, not just filling seats
  • Confident - proud of the new office
  • Minimal - let the design breathe

Reference: lyratechnologies.com.au - match this aesthetic. Dark backgrounds, purple accent glows, clean typography.
