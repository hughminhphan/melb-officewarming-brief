## 3. Brand Guidelines

### Typography

| Typeface | Use | Weight | Source |
|----------|-----|--------|--------|
| **Satoshi** | Primary Lyra brand font — headings, body, web, all materials | Regular (400), Medium (500) | [Fontshare](https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/satoshi) |

> **Note:** Lyra uses **Satoshi** as its sole typeface across all brand materials and its website. Do NOT use Space Grotesk, Basement Grotesque, or Helvetica Light — those belong to a separate personal brand, not Lyra.

**For this poster:** Use **Satoshi** throughout. You may pair it with a complementary geometric sans-serif for display if needed, but Satoshi alone at different weights (Regular vs Medium) should be sufficient. Do NOT use more than 2 typefaces on the poster.

### Colour Palette

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Background** | `#000000` or `#010314` | Dark-mode-first brand. Pure black or very dark navy. |
| **Primary text** | `#D4D4D6` | Light gray on dark backgrounds |
| **Secondary text** | `#9E9EA0` | Mid gray for supporting info |
| **Accent purple** | `#B59BFC` | Key brand accent (use at ~42% opacity for subtle glow) |
| **Gradient accent** | `#9E48FE` → `#2C42E6` | Purple-to-blue gradient — used on CTAs and highlights |
| **Deep purple** | `#421E6B` | Background accent zones |
| **Card/surface** | `#1D1D22` | Dark charcoal for cards/containers |
| **Warm off-white** | `#FAF9F5` | Used sparingly for contrast sections |

**Key principle:** Dark, minimal, premium aesthetic. Lots of negative space. Subtle glassmorphism (frosted glass effects, `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)` borders). The boldness comes from ONE element at a time — not everything screaming.

### Logo

Included in `assets/logos/`:
- `logo-white.png` — White wordmark (use on dark backgrounds) — **981×360px**
- `logo-black.png` — Black wordmark (use on light backgrounds) — **981×360px**
- `logo-icon.png` — Icon mark only — **330×330px**

**Usage:** Logo should be present but not dominant. Bottom or top corner placement. Let the event messaging be the hero.

### Visual Style

- **Dark, minimal, premium** — clean layouts, generous whitespace
- **Glassmorphism accents** — subtle frosted glass effects, translucent borders
- **Border radius:** 12px on cards/containers (rounded, not pill)
- **Photography:** Use real team/office photos where possible (see assets folder)
- **NO stock photos.** Lyra's brand is authentic — use the real office and real people or use abstract/geometric design elements.

## 5. Mood & Direction

### Tone
Celebratory but not corporate. Think "house party invite designed by a tech company" not "corporate event flyer." The vibe is:
- **Warm** — you're genuinely invited, not just filling seats
- **Confident** — we're proud of the new office
- **Minimal** — let the design breathe, don't overcrowd

### Design References
- Lyra's own website: [lyratechnologies.com.au](https://lyratechnologies.com.au) — match this aesthetic
- Dark backgrounds with purple accent glows
- Clean typography with generous spacing
- If using photography, slight gradient overlays to maintain the dark theme

### What NOT to do
- Don't make it look like a corporate annual report
- Don't use bright/neon colours outside the purple accent palette
- Don't overcrowd with text — less is more
- Don't use generic event poster templates
